AMENITIES & SPECIALIZED SERVICES

At the Temple Inn & Suites we take pride at the quality of our instalations and specialized services, which are designed to meet the needs of all of our guests and temple patrons who come to serve in the temple or participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.

Specialized Services

-Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes.

-Playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons.

-Long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries.

-Temple history themed reception and sitting area.

-Wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.

-Family search center that supports family search and ordinance work.
